Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 15, 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At SecureVault Pro, your privacy is our highest priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our password management service.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

Account Information

  • Email address for account creation and communication
  • Username (optional) for personalization
  • Account creation date and last login information
  • Subscription status and billing information (for premium plans)

Usage Data

  • Service access logs for security monitoring
  • Device information for compatibility optimization
  • Feature usage statistics for service improvement
  • Performance metrics and error reports

What We Don't Collect

  • Your master password - Never stored or transmitted
  • Your encrypted passwords - Cannot be accessed by us
  • Personal files or documents - Only password data is stored
  • Sensitive personal information beyond what's necessary

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

SecureVault Pro operates on a zero-knowledge principle:

  • End-to-End Encryption: Your data is encrypted locally on your device before transmission
  • Master Password Privacy: We never have access to your master password
  • No Data Access: We cannot read, access, or recover your encrypted data
  • Client-Side Decryption: Data is only decrypted on your authorized devices

Important: Because of our zero-knowledge architecture, if you forget your master password, we cannot recover your data. This is a security feature that ensures your privacy.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining our password management service
  • Processing subscription payments and managing your account
  • Sending important service notifications and security alerts
  • Responding to your support requests and inquiries
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our service
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing fraud
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is implemented through multiple layers:

Encryption Standards

  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Argon2 key derivation for master password hashing
  • Regular encryption key rotation

Storage Infrastructure

  • Secure cloud storage with redundancy
  • Geographically distributed data centers
  • Regular automated backups
  • Physical security at data facilities

Access Controls

  • Strict employee access limitations
  • Multi-factor authentication for staff
  • Audit logging for all system access
  • Regular security assessments

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Payment processors for subscription billing
  • Cloud infrastructure providers for data storage
  • Security services for fraud prevention
  • All providers are contractually bound to protect your data

Legal Requirements

  • When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity
  • In emergency situations to protect personal safety

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View and export your stored password data at any time
  • Correction: Update your account information and email preferences
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Portability: Export your data in various formats for migration
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Security: Enable two-factor authentication for enhanced protection

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

Essential Cookies

  • Session management for secure login
  • Security token storage
  • Service functionality and preferences

Analytics Cookies

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error tracking and debugging

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Encrypted Passwords: Stored until account deletion
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for security purposes
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Deleted Data: Permanently erased within 30 days of deletion

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in data centers located outside your country. We ensure:

  • Adequate data protection standards in all jurisdictions
  • Compliance with international data transfer regulations
  • Standard contractual clauses with service providers
  • EU-US Privacy Shield framework participation (where applicable)

Children's Privacy

SecureVault Pro is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We will notify users of significant changes via email or through our service. Your continued use of SecureVault Pro after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
